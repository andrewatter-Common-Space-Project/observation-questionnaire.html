Reflection Questions

1. When you rated the pejorative statements, did any of them sound familiar from your own workplace observations? This is completely normal - we all fall into these patterns.

2. What makes evidence-based observation difficult? (Common answers: It takes more time, requires emotional distance, needs specific attention to detail)

3. How might using evidence-based language change your professional conversations? Think about discussions with managers, colleagues, or in formal reviews.

4. Can you rewrite three pejorative statements from your own work experience as evidence-based observations? This is the practice that builds the skill.
